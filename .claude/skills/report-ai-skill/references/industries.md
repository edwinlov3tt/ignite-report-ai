# Industry Benchmarks Reference

## Automotive

### Seasonality
- **Peak**: Q4 (year-end sales events), Memorial Day, Labor Day
- **Low**: Q1 (post-holiday), Early Q3

### Benchmarks
| Platform | Tactic | CPM | CTR | CPA |
|----------|--------|-----|-----|-----|
| Facebook | Retargeting | $12.50 | 1.8% | $45 |
| Facebook | Prospecting | $8.00 | 0.9% | $85 |
| Google Search | - | $2.50 CPC | 4.5% | - |
| Programmatic | Display | $4.00 | 0.12% | - |

### Insights
- Model launch periods see 2-3x higher CPMs
- Local DMA targeting typically outperforms broad geo
- Video content performs well for consideration stage
- Service/parts messaging has different seasonality than sales

### Seasonality

- **Low**: Summer months

### Benchmarks
| Platform | Tactic | CPM | CTR | CPA |

| Facebook | Prospecting | $10.00 | 0.7% | $200 |

### Insights

## E-commerce

### Seasonality
- **Peak**: Q4 (holiday), Prime Day, BFCM
- **Low**: January (post-holiday)

### Benchmarks
| Platform | Tactic | CPM | CTR | ROAS |
|----------|--------|-----|-----|------|
| Facebook | Retargeting | $15.00 | 2.5% | 4.0x |
| Facebook | Prospecting | $9.00 | 1.2% | 2.0x |
| Google Shopping | - | $0.80 CPC | 1.5% | 5.0x |
| Programmatic | Display | $3.00 | 0.15% | - |

### Insights
- ROAS benchmarks vary significantly by price point
- Cart abandonment retargeting typically highest performer
- Holiday CPMs can be 2-4x normal levels
- Product feed quality directly impacts Shopping performance
