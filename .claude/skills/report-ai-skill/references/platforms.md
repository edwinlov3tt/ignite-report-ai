# Platform Knowledge Base

## Facebook/Meta

### Quirks
- Learning phase requires 50 conversions per week per ad set to exit
- iOS 14.5+ tracking limitations significantly affect conversion attribution
- Frequency above 3x in 7 days typically indicates audience saturation
- Advantage+ campaigns may override manual targeting settings
- Attribution window defaults: 7-day click, 1-day view

### Primary KPIs by Objective
| Objective | Primary KPIs |
|-----------|--------------|
| Awareness | CPM, Reach, Frequency, Video Views |
| Traffic | CPC, CTR, Landing Page Views |
| Conversions | CPA, ROAS, Conversion Rate |
| Engagement | Engagement Rate, Post Reactions |

### Warning Thresholds
| Metric | Warning | Critical |
|--------|---------|----------|
| Frequency (7d) | >3.0 | >5.0 |
| CTR | <0.8% | <0.5% |
| CPM | >$20 | >$30 |

## Google Ads

### Quirks
- Quality Score heavily impacts actual CPC and ad position
- Smart Bidding needs 15-30 conversions per month minimum
- Display Network metrics should be analyzed separately from Search
- Auto-applied recommendations can change campaigns unexpectedly
- Data-driven attribution may shift credit between campaigns

### Primary KPIs by Campaign Type
| Type | Primary KPIs |
|------|--------------|
| Search | CPC, CTR, Conversion Rate, Quality Score |
| Display | CPM, Viewable Impressions, CTR |
| Video | CPV, View Rate, Watch Time |
| Shopping | ROAS, CPC, Conversion Value |

### Warning Thresholds
| Metric | Warning | Critical |
|--------|---------|----------|
| Quality Score | <5 | <3 |
| Impression Share | <50% | <30% |
| Search CTR | <2% | <1% |

---

## Programmatic Display

### Quirks
- Viewability standards: 50% in view for 1 second (display)
- Brand safety filters can significantly limit scale
- Private marketplace deals may have minimum spend commitments
- Frequency caps work at DSP level, not across platforms

### Primary KPIs by Format
| Format | Primary KPIs |
|--------|--------------|
| Display | CPM, Viewability Rate, CTR |
| Video | CPCV, Completion Rate, Viewability |
| CTV | Completion Rate, CPM, Reach |
| Audio | Completion Rate, CPM, Listen-Through |

### Warning Thresholds
| Metric | Warning | Critical |
|--------|---------|----------|
| Viewability | <60% | <50% |
| Brand Safety | <95% | <90% |
| Completion Rate | <70% | <60% |
